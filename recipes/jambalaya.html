Jambalaya

New Orleans Style Jambalaya

New Orleans Style Jambalaya is a quick and easy meal that is great for any day of the week. If your short on time, or just want to jazz things up a little this is a great dish to try out.

This dish generally contains your choice of protein mainly being a pork or shrimp embedded in a spiced up bed of rice and vegetables. Some variations of the dish even contain macaroni or a type of pasta!

Ingredients

  • Choice of protein: Sausage, Chicken, or Shrimp
  • Box of Jambalaya rice or dirty rice mix
  • Choice of vegetables: Corn, Peppers, Beans
  • Vegetable Oil
  • Optional: Cajun Seasoning

Steps

  1. Heat a drizzle of vegetable oil on medium head in a deep dish skillet.
  2. Meanwhile cut up your sausage into small slices if using sausage or prepare your shrimp
  3. Wash your vegetables
  4. Next pour two cups of water into the skillet and add meat and vegetables
  5. Turn heat to high and sitr and cover skillet until boiling
  6. Once boiling set the heat to simmer and set a timer for 25 minutes
  7. Every few minutes stir the skillet. With around 15 minutes left add any additional seasonings desired.
  8. After the timer is done remove dish from heat and let cool.
  9. Enjoy! Jambalaya is best paired with some Jazz music and a fruity wine to enhance its spicy and fun flavor.
